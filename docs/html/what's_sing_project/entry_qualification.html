ENTRY QUALIFICATION

PURPSE OF STUDY ABROAD

Main purpose of study aboroad is learning Japanese. You can learn writing and speaking in Japanese. You can also interact Japanese students. So you can make Japanese friends.

DESIRED STUDENT

We want students who want to learn Japanese very hard and be interested in Japanese culture.

QUALIFICATION

You must be AFREI student and your grade must be from first to third. And your TOEIC score must be no less than 600. Student who meet their conditions can join our program.
