Message from the President of GUFS

Gokiso University of foreign studies has growing as one of the leading foreign colleges in Japan. GUFS makes every effort to support individuals who can broadcast themselves confidently. We offer the educational style best suited to each student’s ability, talent, and intention. At GUFS, we realize each student’s hidden communication abilities and develop them so that students can communicate correctly and surely. GUFS is a renowned international university with 140 instructors who are native speakers of foreign languages (over 50% of all our instructors). Japanese students, foreign instructors, and students from all over the world are gathered at the GUFS global campus. Our goal is that students have skills which facilitate creative discussion toward problem solving.
